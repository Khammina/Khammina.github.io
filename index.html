I'm Khammina SCMV — a multidisciplinary MEPF Technical Engineer, Shop Drawing Draftsman, and Algorithmic Trading Adviser based in Vientiane, Laos.

With over six years of hands-on experience in the construction and engineering industry, I specialise in the preparation of precise Shop Drawings and Technical Drawings for Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Protection (MEPF), and LPG systems. My work spans the full project lifecycle — from concept design and coordination drawings through to as-built documentation — ensuring every deliverable meets international standards and client specifications.

I am equally passionate about graphic design and front-end development, which allows me to bridge the gap between technical precision and visual communication — delivering solutions that are both functional and aesthetically refined.
